Introduction

These are 10 ways to conserve water in your household! Each of these ways are very useful and you can use these to save water and help the planet!

Way 1

Way 1: Using a dishwasher, Instead of washing dishes with hands, you can use a dishwasher because it uses less water compared to hand-washing. .

Way 2

Way 2: Turn off the water. While brushing, bathing, and showering, try to conserve water, instead of wasting it. Use water wisely and don’t waste it recklessly. .

Way 3

Way 3: Install your washing machine wisely. Instead of spending so much money on top load washers which are more expensive than front-load washers, you can spend 75% of that money to buy a front-load washer because front-load washers use less water than Top-load washers. .

Way 4

Way 4: Water your plants wisely. Water your grass, lawn, or your garden prior to sunrise,or after sunset because after the sun rises, the water evaporates immediately and there is no use of the water and it’s wasted. If you do it after the sunset, then the water will last till the morning, and it’s used properly. .

Way 5

Way 5: When cooking, use the proper amount of water instead of pouring huge amounts of it. This way, you can save water and this will also help to keep more nutrients. .

Way 6

Way 6: Wash your pets in your lawn, then all the rinsed water that you used to clean your pet(s) will go to your lawn and will be used properly..

Way 7

Way 7: Instead of replacing your pool water every time and wasting a lot of water, install a pool cover, this way your chemicals and bacteria won’t enter the pool, and the pool will be clean at all times..

Way 8

Way 8: In the bathroom, instead of flushing toilet paper, throw them in the trash, this can save water! .

Way 9

Way 9: When washing your vegetables and fruits, don’t waste the water by throwing out the rinsed water, use it to water plants in your household. .

Way 10

Way 10: When you put ice cubes in your drink, and they are left out because you drank the drink but the ice cubes didn’t melt, instead of throwing them out, pour them on a plant. .

Finishing Up

The end! You can definetely use these ways in your home and help save water and protect our planet!!
